TEAMS

Teams are pretty snazzy, but what are they exactly?

  • Joining a team puts you at a great advantage.
  • All coins and other materials will be shared among the team.
  • The team will always be surfing together, as you will always be spawned on the same map.
  • As a human, you will be sharing and building upon a mutual house.
  • A maximum of four players per team.
  • Good luck and fight together!

Warnings

  • Join a team and all your coins and materials will be transfered to the team supplies.
  • Should you choose to leave a team, you will have to start over without any supplies.
  • Can be very addicting :)
